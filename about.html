Welcome to OurSulap

The Home Away From Home

OurSulap is a cozy homestay that offers comfort and convenience at an unbeatable price. Located in the heart of the city, it is easy to access and has all the amenities you need for a pleasant stay.

OurSulap offers 3 comfortable rooms, 1 toilet and 1 kitchen which has everything you need to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable. Plus, with our low prices, you won't have to worry about breaking your bank!

Make Memories That Last
With OurSulap, you will be able to make beautiful memories that will last a lifetime. Whether it's spending time with family or friends, or simply just enjoying some alone time - we have something for everyone!
